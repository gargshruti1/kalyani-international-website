About Us

We Provide Best Range of Food Products

Incorporated in the year 2000, Kalyani International is considered amongst the famous manufacturer, wholesaler and trader of an extensive range of Dried Kachri, Cumin Seeds, Dry Mint Leaves and much more.

Being a quality-centric organization, we strive hard to maintain the quality in our offered range of Kasuri Methi, Ker Sangri, Dried Ker, Dried Guar, Dried Kachri etc. To make our products in line with international quality standards, we have set-up a fully functional quality control center that is staffed by an adroit team of professionals.

We have put in place a sound system of quality assurance, which helps us in ensuring a reliable and effective way of controlling our overall manufacturing processes. In order to achieve this goal, we offer these products in different technical specifications and can be customized as per the needs of the clients.
